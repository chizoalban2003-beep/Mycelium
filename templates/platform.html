Platform flow
  1. Device signals are captured from app use, telemetry, chat, and approved actions.
  2. The signals are flattened into safe structured rows.
  3. The physics predictor and viscosity layer decide timing and flow.
  4. The governance layer checks consent and trust before actions.
  5. The UI surfaces the result as a visible learning trail and summary.
  6. Hive shows approved nodes and shared wisdom in real time.
